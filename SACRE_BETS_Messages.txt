SACRÉ BETS — How to Play
=========================

OBJECTIVE
---------
You have 250 points to bet across 26 questions about who's most likely to do something during the Montreal trip. Bet smart, read the group, and finish with the highest score.


1. BEFORE THE TRIP — PLACE YOUR BETS
-------------------------------------
- There are 26 "Who is most likely to..." questions.
- For each question, pick one person you think will do it and bet points on them (minimum 5 points per question).
- You do NOT have to bet on every question — spread your 250 points however you want.
- You can bet on yourself.
- Once you're done, lock in your bets. After locking, bets cannot be changed unless the admin unlocks everyone.
- All bets are placed BEFORE the trip. No live betting.


2. AFTER BETTING CLOSES — KEY TERMS
------------------------------------
Once everyone has locked in, the system calculates these for each question:

- The Favorite — The person who received the most total points bet on them for that question. This is who the group collectively thinks will do it.
- The Underdog — Anyone who is NOT the favorite. If you bet on someone who isn't the favorite, you're making an underdog bet.
- The Pot — The total points bet on the favorite for that question.
- Challenge Value — 50% of the Pot. This is what the favorite personally stands to win or lose.

Example: For a question, Rahul gets the most bets totaling 60 pts. Favorite = Rahul, Pot = 60, Challenge Value = 30.


3. DURING THE TRIP — THREE OUTCOMES
------------------------------------
Each question is resolved during the trip in one of three ways:

A. THE FAVORITE DOES IT
  - Anyone who bet on the favorite earns 1.5x their bet
  - The favorite personally earns +50% of the Pot (bonus for delivering)
  - Everyone else — no effect
  Example: Pot = 60. You bet 10 on the favorite → +15. The favorite → +30 bonus.

B. SOMEONE ELSE DOES IT (UNDERDOG STEALS IT)
  This is the big swing outcome:
  - Anyone who bet on the favorite loses their full bet
  - Anyone who correctly bet on the actual person earns 2.5x their bet
  - The favorite loses 50% of the Pot (penalty for choking)
  - The person who actually did it earns 75% of the Pot (underdog bonus)
  - Everyone who bet on someone else (wrong) — no effect
  Example: Pot = 60. You bet 10 on the favorite → -10. You bet 10 on the actual person → +25. The favorite → -30. The person who did it → +45.

C. NOBODY DOES IT
  - Anyone who bet on the favorite loses their full bet
  - The favorite loses 50% of the Pot
  - Everyone else — no effect
  Example: Pot = 60. You bet 10 on the favorite → -10. The favorite → -30.


4. HOW SCORING WORKS
---------------------
Points move in two independent ways. You can earn from BOTH on the same question.

A. Bet Results (based on who you bet on):
  Correct bet on the favorite        → +1.5x your bet
  Correct bet on a non-favorite      → +2.5x your bet
  Wrong bet on the favorite          → lose full bet
  Wrong bet on anyone else           → no effect

B. Personal Results (if you're personally involved):
  You're the favorite & you deliver      → +50% of the Pot
  You're the favorite & you don't deliver → -50% of the Pot
  You're the underdog & you steal it     → +75% of the Pot


5. HOW TO WIN
--------------
After all 26 questions are resolved, add up all your gains and losses — from your bets, your personal bonuses, and your personal penalties. The player with the highest total score wins.

PAGES IN THE APP
-----------------
- Place Bets — Where you pick a player and bet points for each question. This is the only page where you make choices.
- The Odds Board — See every question's favorite, pot size, and who the group is betting on. Use this to spot big pots you might steal.
- What's At Stake — Your personal breakdown. For each question, see exactly how much you win or lose under every possible outcome. Also shows if you're the favorite or have an underdog opportunity.
- Who's Winning — The leaderboard. Shows total scores, who's been the favorite, who delivered, who choked, and who hijacked questions.
- Judgment Day — (Admin only) Where the admin resolves each question during the trip.
